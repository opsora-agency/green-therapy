About Green Therapy

Know who we are and why we do this

Who We Are

Green Therapy is a compassionate space for emotional healing, stress relief, and inner balance. Led by Mukulsinhji Chavda (MPharm), the approach blends scientific clarity with soulful communication to gently release emotional heaviness and professional burnout.

Unlike conventional therapy that may focus on diagnosis and medication, Green Therapy offers a unique word-based healing experience. Through deep, empathetic conversations, we help you release what no longer serves you — without any medication, without any judgment.

Our Philosophy

We believe that true healing happens when someone truly listens. Not just to your words, but to what lies beneath them — the unspoken worries, the hidden burdens, the quiet pains that words often fail to capture.

In a world that constantly pushes us to do more, be more, and achieve more, we forget to pause. We forget to breathe. We forget that it's okay to not be okay. Green Therapy is that pause — a gentle reminder that you don't have to carry everything alone.

Our approach is simple: compassionate listening, gentle guidance, and complete acceptance. No pressure. No rushing. Just you, a safe space, and someone who truly cares.

Our Approach

What makes Green Therapy different? We don't follow rigid protocols or textbook techniques. Every person is unique, and so is their healing journey. We meet you exactly where you are — whether you're dealing with daily stress, deep emotional wounds, or complete burnout.

Our sessions are conversational, not clinical. We talk. We listen. We reflect. And through this gentle process, clarity emerges, heaviness lifts, and you begin to find your way back to yourself.

  • 100% word-based healing — no medication
  • Non-judgmental, compassionate space
  • Move entirely at your own pace
  • No preparation needed — come as you are

About Mukulsinhji Chavda

Mukulsinhji Chavda holds a Master's degree in Pharmacy (MPharm) in Pharmaceutical Sciences. As a pharmacist, academic, and emotional healing guide, he brings a unique perspective to mental wellness — one that bridges the gap between scientific understanding and soulful connection.

Having worked in high-pressure academic and pharmaceutical environments, he deeply understands the weight of professional burnout, the exhaustion of constant striving, and the quiet desperation that often goes unnoticed. This personal and professional experience shapes his empathetic, grounded approach to healing.

"If you have acidity, I can carry your acidity without giving any — only with the help of words," he says. This simple statement captures the essence of Green Therapy: compassionate listening as medicine.
