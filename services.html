Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Green Therapy

Q.

What is Green Therapy?

Green Therapy is a compassionate, word-based emotional healing approach. No medication — only deep, empathetic conversations that help you release stress, anxiety, and emotional heaviness.

Q.

How is this different from traditional therapy?

Traditional therapy often involves multiple sessions and techniques. Green Therapy focuses specifically on word-based healing in a gentle, non-judgmental space. You don't need to prepare anything — just come as you are.

Q.

Do I need any medical diagnosis?

No. You don't need any diagnosis or medical history. Whether you're dealing with stress, anxiety, burnout, or just feeling overwhelmed — you're welcome here.

Q.

How long is a session?

Each session is 45–60 minutes, conducted over video or voice call. We move entirely at your pace — no rushing, no pressure.

Q.

Is this available worldwide?

Yes. All sessions are conducted online, so you can join from anywhere in the world. All time zones are welcome.

Q.

How do I book my first session?

Simply send a message on WhatsApp or email. No forms, no lengthy processes — just reach out and we'll find a time that works for you.

Q.

Is it confidential?

Absolutely. Every conversation is completely confidential and private. Your healing journey stays between us.
